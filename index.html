MIRROR
WINDOW

A window mirror is a decorative item that resembles a windowpane, typically featuring multiple panes or sections like a traditional window. Instead of transparent glass, though, it usually has reflective surfaces, such as mirrors, within each pane. This creates the illusion of a window while also serving as a mirror for practical use. Window mirrors come in various styles, sizes, and materials, from rustic wooden frames to sleek, modern metal designs. They're often used to add depth, light, and a sense of space to a room, making them popular in interior design for both aesthetic and functional purposes.
